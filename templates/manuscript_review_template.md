## Review Dimensions

### 1. Narrative Arc and Pacing
- Does the book build logically from introduction to conclusion?
- Are there pacing problems: too fast, too slow, repetitive, or underdeveloped?
- Is the peak insight placed appropriately?
- Does the manuscript maintain investigative momentum without melodrama?

### 2. Cross-Chapter Continuity
- Any contradictions between chapters that were not caught by per-chapter review?
- Are recurring themes developed consistently throughout?
- Are cross-references accurate, rare, and organic?
- Are core concepts introduced before they are relied upon?
**Notes:** {{}}

### 3. Voice Consistency
- Does the voice feel consistent across all chapters and logues?
- Any jarring shifts in tone, register, or style?
- Does the prose maintain calm alarm, translation over sensationalism, and narrative clarity?
- Does the narrator remain the lens, not the subject?
**Notes:** {{}}

### 4. Redundancy and Repetition
- Are key concepts introduced once and then developed rather than re-explained?
- Do any chapters overlap significantly in content?
- Are metaphors, examples, or vignettes repeated too often?
**Notes:** {{}}

### 5. Gaps and Missing Content
- Is any Book Bible requirement underdeveloped or missing?
- Are there logical jumps where more explanation is needed?
- Are source-sensitive claims framed with appropriate caution?
**Notes:** {{}}

### 6. Opening and Closing Strength
- Do Foreword, Introduction, and Prologue serve distinct purposes without redundancy?
- Does the final chapter resolve the book's central argument?
- Does the Epilogue provide satisfying closure without introducing major new evidence?
- Does the Glossary, if included, work as concise back matter?
**Notes:** {{}}

### 7. Production Formatting Audit
- No horizontal rules in chapters or logues.
- No chapter-body `##` or `###` subheadings.
- No italic subtitle lines directly after chapter/logue titles.
- No citation markers, footnotes, bibliography-style references, or bold body prose.
- No excessive explicit chapter-number references.
- Clean paragraph spacing and consistent top-level headings.
